Smash Burgers

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • salt, pepper, garlic and onion powder, paprika, Italian seasoning
  • Sour cream
  • Crispy chopped up bacon
  • Around a quarter cup of pickle juice
  • Whatever you want to assemble your burger

    • Instructions

    Take 1 pound of thawed out ground beef, mix it in a bowl with salt, pepper, garlic and onion powder, papkrika, and Italian seasoning. Now add pickle juice, sour cream, and chopped up and cooked bacon. Mix and make into meatballs onto a tin foil pan, bake in oven at 350 degrees for 20 minutes. When done, take out of oven and baste, then let rest. Now make a burger with anything else you want like lettuce, cheese, sauce.
