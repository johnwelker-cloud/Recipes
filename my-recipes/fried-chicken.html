Fried Chicken

Ingredients

  • Raw chicken
  • Butter milk
  • 2 eggs
  • Salt, pepper, garlic and onion powder, paprika, sazon, old bay, Italian seasoning, caijun seasoning
  • flour
  • cornstarch
  • Frying oil

    • Instructions

    Take your preferred amount of eaither chicken tenderloines or wings and get them ready to soak in a batter for 3-5 hours- a day. To make the batter, put the chicken in a big bowl, put enough butter milk to cover all chicken, now add 2 eggs. Now, add salt, pepper, garlic powder, onion powder, Italian seasoning, paprika, old bay, sazon, and caijun seasoning. Mix well and cover with plastic wrap and let sit in fridge for a couple hours or a day at most. After marinating, take out chicken and coat it in a mixture of flour, cornstarch, and all the same seasonings from earlier. Heat oil up to 350 degrees and fry for around 7-10 minutes. After turning golden brown, add a pinch of salt and let sit, now eat with a sauce of your choice.
